FAQ


What is GreenBean ?

Green Bean is a cozy coffee shop located at the heart of the UCL Bloomsbury Campus.

What is this site used for ?

This site can be used to order coffee from GreenBean online, then you can go pick it up afterwards, avoiding the lines !

How do I order?

Please go to the 'Shop' Tab, where you can find all the available products. Once you have decided on what to order
press "Add to cart", the selected product will be added to your cart. Go to the Cart tab, where you will find a list
of products in your cart. After checking that you have what you want click checkout and follow on-screen instructions !
